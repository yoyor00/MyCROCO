License compatibility
---------------------
All of these licenses are compatible with CeCILL-2.1:
- MIT is permissive (compatible with everything).
- CeCILL-C allows integration into a CeCILL-2.1 work (article 5.3.4).
- GPLv2+ is compatible with CeCILL-2.1 (CeCILL-2.1, article 5.3.4).
- AGRIF/LIB contains files generated by GNU Bison 2.7; these carry the
  Bison exception which permits distribution under any terms.

Full license texts for all third-party components are in the LICENSES/
directory.
